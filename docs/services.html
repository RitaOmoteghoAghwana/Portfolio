FAQ

The questions I always get.

01

Do I need to provide content before we start?

+
Ideally, yes — copy, logos, and brand assets speed everything up. But I can also write first-draft copy and source stock visuals as part of the Shine and Diamond packages. We'll discuss this on the discovery call.
02

Where are you based and what timezone do you work in?

+
I'm based in Lagos, Nigeria (WAT, UTC+1) and work with clients worldwide. I overlap with EU mornings and US East Coast afternoons. I reply within 24 hours, usually faster.
03

Will the AI music video look "AI-y"?

+
It'll look stylized — that's the point. I lean into the medium rather than trying to fake live-action. Think music-video moodboards, not deepfakes. You'll see references on the discovery call so there are no surprises.
04

Do you book on Fiverr or directly?

+
Both. Fiverr is great for first-time clients who want the platform's buyer protection. Direct booking (via this site or LinkedIn) is faster, slightly cheaper, and gives us more flexibility on scope.
05

What if I'm not happy with the result?

+
Every package includes 2–3 revision rounds. Beyond that, I'll keep iterating at a per-hour rate. So far I've never had a project end without the client happy — but if it ever did, we'd refund the unbuilt portion. No drama.
06

Can I see references for the kind of WordPress sites you build?

+
Yes — drop me a note via the contact form and I'll send a curated case-study PDF with five live sites, the brief behind each, and the outcomes. Easier than a portfolio scroll.
07

Do you handle hosting and domains?

+
I don't resell hosting (you should own that directly), but I'll guide you through buying a domain and choosing hosting that won't bite you later. I deploy to your account.
