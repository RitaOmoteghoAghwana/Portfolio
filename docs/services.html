FAQ

Questions I always get.

01

Do I need to provide content before we start?

+
Ideally yes — logos, brand colors and any copy speed things up. But I can write first-draft copy and source visuals for the Shine and Diamond packages. We'll sort it on the discovery call.
02

How do payments work?

+
For direct bookings I take 50% upfront to begin and 50% on delivery. Fiverr handles payments automatically through their platform with buyer protection built in.
03

Will the AI video look like an AI video?

+
It'll look stylized — that's the point and why it stands out. I lean into the medium rather than faking live-action. You'll see mood-board references before I generate a single frame.
04

Can I book on Fiverr or only directly?

+
Both. Fiverr is great for buyer protection on your first order. Direct booking via this site is slightly faster and gives more flexibility on scope and add-ons.
05

What if I'm not happy with the result?

+
Every package includes 2–3 revision rounds. I keep iterating until it feels right. I've never had a project close unhappy — but if yours ever did, we'd refund the undelivered portion, no drama.
06

Do you handle hosting and domains?

+
I don't resell hosting — you should own it directly. I'll guide you to the right host for your budget, help you set it up, and deploy everything to your account.
